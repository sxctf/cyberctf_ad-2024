[V.B0ARD] - Visual Board



The service is designed to display the movement of a train along the route, control the display of information and advertising messages on train screens (boards). The service also provides a loyalty program for customers (promo codes). Users of the service can submit applications for the placement of information and advertising messages on train screens (boards) indicating the title and main part of the message, as well as a serial key (if available, issued after the conclusion of the relevant agreement), which provides priority when considering applications by the train administration.
